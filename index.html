About Latent

We're building the brain
every company deserves.

A company born in the factory.
Built for every enterprise.

诞生于工厂
服务于所有企业

Latent started from a simple observation: decision-makers spend more time reconciling data across 5+ systems than actually deciding. Systems don't talk. Teams burn out. Opportunities slip. And nobody has a complete picture until the quarterly review.

Latent 源于一个简单的观察：决策者花在"跨 5+ 个系统对数据"上的时间，远多于真正做决策的时间。系统互不连通，团队过载，机会溜走，等到季度评审才看见完整问题。

We started in manufacturing R&D because that's where the pain is sharpest. But the brain we're building applies to every org that runs on fragmented systems — which is every org.

我们从制造业研发起步——那是痛点最尖锐的地方。但我们构建的这颗大脑适用于所有靠多个系统运转的组织——也就是所有组织。
