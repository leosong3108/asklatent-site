About Latent
关于 Latent

We're building the operating
system for manufacturing R&D.

我们在打造
制造业研发的操作系统

A company born in the factory.

一家诞生在工厂里的公司

Latent started from a simple observation: manufacturing R&D leaders spend more time reconciling data across 5 systems than making decisions. The systems don't talk. Engineers burn out. Projects slip. And nobody has a complete picture until the quarterly review.

Latent 的起点来自一个观察：制造业研发管理者花在"在 5 个系统之间对数据"上的时间，远多于真正做决策的时间。系统互不连通，工程师过载，项目延期，等到季度评审才发现完整问题。

We're building the intelligence layer that makes all of that visible in real time — not through another dashboard, but through the oldest interface in history: asking a question and getting a straight answer.

我们在做的，是让这一切实时可见——不是通过又一个看板，而是通过人类最古老的接口：问一个问题，得到一个直接的答案。
