How does Latent connect to our existing systems? Latent 如何连接我们现有的系统？
Pre-built connectors for SAP, Oracle, Siemens, PTC, Dassault, Yonyou, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Confluence, WeCom, Feishu, DingTalk — plus REST and SQL bridges for anything with an API. Custom connectors for legacy or in-house systems are included in enterprise onboarding at no extra cost.
预制连接器覆盖 SAP、Oracle、西门子、PTC、达索、用友、GitHub、GitLab、Jira、Confluence、企业微信、飞书、钉钉——加上 REST 和 SQL 桥接层，只要有 API 的系统都能接入。老旧或自研系统可定制连接器，企业版实施包内免费提供。
Can Latent write back to our source systems? Latent 会写回到我们的源系统吗？
No, read-only by default. If you want write-back — e.g. updating a Jira ticket or closing a PLM milestone — it requires explicit per-user authorization, is scoped to specific actions, and every write is logged. Your systems remain the source of truth.
默认只读。如果你需要写回——比如更新 Jira 工单或关闭 PLM 里程碑——需要明确的用户级授权，限定具体动作，每次写入都有完整日志。你的系统始终是唯一真实来源。
How do you handle permissions and access control? 权限和访问控制怎么处理？
Latent inherits permissions from your source systems. If a user can't see a project in PLM, they can't ask Latent about it either. Integrates with SSO (Okta, Azure AD, 飞书身份), SCIM for user provisioning, and your existing RBAC policies. No separate permission layer to maintain.
Latent 继承源系统的权限。如果用户在 PLM 看不到某个项目，也问不了 Latent。支持 SSO（Okta、Azure AD、飞书身份），SCIM 用户同步，以及你现有的 RBAC 策略。不需要维护独立的权限层。
What about AI hallucinations? How accurate are the answers? AI 幻觉怎么办？答案有多准确？
Every answer is grounded in real data from your systems — with clickable citations for every claim. Users can trace any statement back to the source record (a specific PLM entry, a Jira ticket, an ERP transaction). When data is insufficient or conflicting, Latent says I don't have enough data to answer this confidently rather than guess.
每个答案都基于你系统里的真实数据——每条结论都有可点击的引用。用户可以追溯到具体的源记录（某条 PLM 记录、某个 Jira 工单、某笔 ERP 交易）。当数据不足或存在冲突时，Latent 会说"数据不足以给出可靠答案"，而不是编造。
How long does deployment take? 部署需要多长时间？
Typical timeline: 48 hours to first insight on a pilot dataset, 2 weeks to full production rollout across your primary systems. Our implementation team is on-site or remote with your team during the first week. Enterprise rollouts with complex legacy systems can run 4–6 weeks.
典型时间线：48 小时在 pilot 数据集上跑出第一批洞察，2 周完成主要系统的正式上线。第一周我们的实施团队驻场或远程跟你们团队一起工作。涉及复杂遗留系统的企业级部署通常 4-6 周。
Can we use our own LLM or keep AI inference on-prem? 可以用我们自己的 LLM 或本地部署 AI 吗？
Yes. Latent runs with your choice of model: open-source (Qwen, DeepSeek, Llama) deployed entirely inside your network, your private cloud LLM (Azure OpenAI, AWS Bedrock), or managed API. For regulated environments, full on-prem inference means no tokens ever leave your infrastructure.
支持。你可以自选模型：开源模型（通义千问、DeepSeek、Llama）完全在你的内网运行，或接入你的私有云 LLM（Azure OpenAI、AWS Bedrock），或托管 API。对于受监管环境，完全本地推理意味着 token 永远不离开你的基础设施。
What's the pricing model? 定价模式？
Enterprise annual contracts priced by number of seats, connected systems, and deployment model (cloud vs. on-prem). Pilot programs are available at a reduced scope to prove value before full rollout. Book a demo for a quote tailored to your environment.
企业级年度合约，按席位数、连接系统数、部署方式（云或本地）定价。提供缩小范围的 pilot 方案，让你先验证价值再全面推开。预约 demo 我们给出针对你环境的报价。
